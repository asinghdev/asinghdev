The Challenge

A leading mining equipment manufacturer faced several challenges in managing their equipment distribution and inventory:

  • Complex supply chain with multiple stakeholders
  • High inventory holding costs
  • Delayed equipment availability at mine sites
  • Manual ordering and fulfillment processes
  • Limited visibility into equipment usage patterns
  • Inefficient maintenance scheduling

Our Solution

We developed a comprehensive marketplace platform that revolutionized mining equipment distribution:

Manufacturers/Sellers Mine Owners ↓ ↓ Marketplace Platform (Cloud-Based) ↓ ↓ Inventory Management Smart Vending ↓ ↓ Analytics Engine IoT Integration ↓ ↓ Predictive Supply Usage Tracking

Key Features:

  • Real-time inventory tracking across locations
  • Smart vending machines for critical supplies
  • Automated reordering system
  • Usage analytics and forecasting
  • Supplier-buyer matching algorithm
  • Mobile app for field operations
